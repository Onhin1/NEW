Shipping and Returns

At Nationwide Distributors & More, we aim to provide a seamless shopping experience by offering transparent and customer-friendly shipping and return policies. Please find the details below:

Shipping:

We offer FREE standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the United States. For orders below $50, a flat shipping rate of $4.99 will apply. Expedited shipping options are available at an additional cost during checkout. Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days and standard shipping takes 3-7 business days for delivery. Tracking information will be provided once your order has been shipped.

Returns:

We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange. Returned items must be in their original condition, unused, and with all packaging and tags attached. To initiate a return, please contact our customer support at Admin@ofpmv.com or call us at (123) 456-7890 with your order number and reason for return. Once your return is approved, we will provide you with a prepaid shipping label and instructions for sending the item back. Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.


Please feel free to reach out to our customer support team if you have any questions or concerns regarding our shipping and return policies.
We're here to help and ensure your shopping experience with Nationwide Distributors & More is smooth and enjoyable.
